Satay Capitol 1951 – Warisan

For satay enthusiasts, there is another destination that you shouldn't miss to experience the deliciousness of their satay. The beef satay is incredibly tender and has a more flavorful spice blend compared to most other satay stalls. The peanut sauce is also tasty and not overly spicy. In addition to satay, they also sell fried food and soto, which are equally popular here. There are two branches available in Kuala Lumpur, one in Pusat Penjaja Selera Muda, Kampung Baru, and the other in Capital Cafe, Jalan TAR. You can visit whichever branch is closest to you.

Nasi Ayam Hainan Taman Melawati

The menu is simple, and the location of the restaurant is quite ordinary, but the reception during lunchtime is exceptionally crowded. This eatery is located in Medan Selera Taman Melawati and managed by a Chinese convert couple who have been established in KL for over 30 years. It offers affordable and delicious Hainanese chicken rice for those who want to personally experience its scrumptiousness. The restaurant operates every day except Thursdays, starting from 11 am until the food runs out. Typically, everything is sold out before Asr prayer time.

Warung Satay Zaini

Warung Satay Zaini is a renowned satay establishment in Kuala Lumpur. With over 35 years of existence, it is considered one of the closest competitors to the famous Satay Kajang. Located on Jalan Kolam Ayer Lama in Ampang Jaya, Kuala Lumpur, this eatery offers a delightful array of satay options.

Mak Teh Ikan Bakar Pasar Dato’ Keramat

Mak Teh Ikan Bakar Pasar Dato' Keramat specializes in preparing fresh fish and grilling it to perfection. They offer a variety of fish options, such as stingray, snapper, tilapia, and more. The fish is marinated in a flavorful blend of spices and then grilled over charcoal, resulting in a smoky and aromatic dish.

Nasi Lemak Antarabangsa

Nasi Lemak Antarabangsa is a renowned nasi lemak brand in Kuala Lumpur, known for its popularity and long-standing presence since the 1970s. It was created by Puan Normi and has received extensive coverage on local television programs, further cementing its reputation.

Gerai Seri Menanti Ikan Bakar Jalan Bellamy

Gerai Seri Menanti Ikan Bakar is known for its casual and laid-back atmosphere, attracting both locals and tourists. The eatery is often frequented by those seeking a satisfying and authentic ikan bakar experience in Kuala Lumpur.
