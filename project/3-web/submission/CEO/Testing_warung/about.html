We Are Warung Hunter

Warung Hunter is a renowned food establishment that has earned a reputation as the best warung-style eatery in Malaysia. With a rich culinary heritage and a commitment to quality, Warung Hunter has become a must-visit destination for both locals and tourists seeking an authentic Malaysian dining experience.At Warung Hunter, the focus is on serving delectable traditional dishes that are true to the essence of Malaysian cuisine. The menu boasts a diverse selection of mouthwatering options, ranging from fragrant nasi lemak and savoury rendang to spicy laksa and crispy roti canai. Each dish is carefully prepared using traditional recipes and fresh, locally sourced ingredients, ensuring an explosion of flavours with every bite.
